Our mission

Community defense, consciousness-raising, and solidarity building

With the rising tide of anti-Asian violence, we organize materially around the thesis of physical safety. We are for the democratization of self-defense, where women, minorities, and LGBTQ+ have meaningful ownership over their lives. We fundraise for and distribute safety gear, offer community self-defense classes through our sister organization Dragon Combat Club, and advocate for initiatives that decentralize knowledge and empower ordinary people, such as Stop The Bleed courses or harm reduction efforts. None of our safety efforts are limited to just Asian Americans. In the past, we've assisted with event security, provided gear to LGBTQ+ community members, and held vigil at sites targeted by right-wing violence.

As a community in diaspora, our broader mission is to Our contributors create and distribute a wide range of educational content, with a focus on Asian history and social justice. We make short posts on our Instagram Page as well as create zines, essays, and other resources to educate a broad audience of Asian Americans and our allies.

As a group, we're interested in building long-term, sustainable solidarity with like-minded organizations across borders and boundaries.
